Privacy Ethics.

01. Zero-Log Protocol

At InstaGrab, we implement a strict zero-log policy. We do not store, track, or monitor your media extraction requests. Your downloads are processed through ephemeral memory sessions that are cleared every 60 seconds.

02. Data Security

All communications between your device and our extraction nodes are encrypted using 256-bit SSL technology. We never share session data with third-party advertisers.

03. Cookie Usage

We use minimal, essential cookies only to manage download rate-limiting and ensure service stability. We do not use tracking pixels for user profiling.
